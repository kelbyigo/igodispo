FAQ

Common questions.

How fast do you close?
Most deals close in 10–21 days depending on title and buyer funding. We give you a realistic timeline at underwriting.
What if the deal falls through?
If a buyer backs out, we re-market immediately at no cost to you. If underwriting shows the deal doesn't work, we tell you within 48 hours.
Who handles earnest money and title?
We coordinate with title on the buyer side. You keep your seller-side EMD arrangement intact.
Assignment or double close?
Depends on the state, the seller, and the buyer. We make the call deal-by-deal and handle the paperwork.
What markets are you strongest in?
We close nationwide, but our deepest buyer pools are in the Southeast, Texas, Arizona, and the Midwest.
What's the minimum deal size?
No hard minimum, but deals with assignment fees under $5K usually aren't worth the time for either of us.
